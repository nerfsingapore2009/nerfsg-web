# NerfSG Hub app screenshots

Drop portrait screenshots of the real NerfSG Hub app here. They appear
automatically in the auto-advancing carousel inside the phone frame on the
homepage "Your games, on your phone" section.

## Guidelines
- **Format:** `.jpg`, `.webp`, `.png`, or `.avif`
- **Aspect:** portrait phone screenshots (roughly 9:19.5 / 1080×2340). They are
  object-fit cover'd into the device frame, so consistent dimensions look best.
- **Order:** files load alphabetically — name them `01-home.jpg`, `02-rsvp.jpg`,
  `03-leaderboard.jpg`, etc. to control the sequence.
- 3–6 screenshots works well.

Until real screenshots are added, the section falls back to a polished mock
phone UI (production) or dev-only placeholders (local only).
